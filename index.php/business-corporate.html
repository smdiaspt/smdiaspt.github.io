Planning a business or a corporate VIP event in Portugal does not need to be stressful.

Absolutely Portugal is pleased to be able to offer a first class service for your travel-based motivational event – incentives, reward trips, meetings, conferences, conventions, presentations, team building and activity days, product launches or other special events, anywhere in Portugal. Our professional team will provide everything you need for a successful event, including venue search and a full range of entertainment ideas for partners as well – such as pre & post-event dinners & social programmes, to enhance your event and to ensure you and your guests enjoy a truly memorable event.

From indulging loyal customers in a luxury weekend dining in legendary castles, rewarding your team for reaching outstanding sales or production targets with amazing experiences such as golfing, sailing or wine tasting, or even entertaining conference partners with exclusive hospitality opportunities like booking concerts, opera, theatre, museums, sight-seeing tours, shopping sprees, sports activities and gourmet diners set in magnificent surroundings such as a stately home, castle or chateaux, we have the experience and creativity to devise and deliver a bespoke solution.

Absolutely Portugal designs each programme with you and your target audience in mind, using our unrivalled skill and extensive experience to meet your requirements.
