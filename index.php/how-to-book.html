Planning your trip with Absolutely Portugal

All great trips start with an idea. If you have a general idea what type of trip you want, where you might want to go and how long you would like to go for, you have all we need to start the process of building your personalised travel itinerary. Our team members will help you plan your exclusive tailor-made trip.

1. Inspiration and information
The website offers inspiration and information, but there’s nothing to match the direct experience of our team. Please use the online questionnaire as a starting point to help us customise your itinerary to your interests, requirements, budget and lifestyle.


2. Speak to a team member
Our team members have the first-hand experience and up-to-date knowledge required to put your plans into action – they can answer your questions, offer suggestions and advice and, if you are ready, start planning your trip in detail. The same team member will handle your trip from start to finish and will be happy to refine your itinerary until you are completely happy.


3. Confirmation and quotation
After our initial conversation, we will send you a quotation and ask you to complete a booking form, agree with the terms and conditions, and pay your non-refundable deposit, at which point we start to confirm all the arrangements. The balance will be paid approximately 4 weeks before your departure.


4. Full itinerary
You will receive a detailed and unique itinerary, complete with a brochure about Portugal and any additional information as requested – from restaurant recommendations to notes on cultural events. We want to ensure that you are able to make the most of your time in Portugal and interact in a positive way with the people you will meet.


5. Staying in touch
Even after booking you’ll undoubtedly think of more questions. Feel free to contact us at any time. In any case we will contact you a few days before you are due to travel, to check everything is OK.
