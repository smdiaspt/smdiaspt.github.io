Ideally situated cottage in the idyllic and secluded fishing village of Arrifana. The cottage offers magnificent ocean views and the beach is one of Europe`s most attractive surfing venues, well known for its reef-breaks. You will also be delighted with the impressive natural environment, dominated by the Southwest Natural Park, with towering cliffs, vast stretches of pristine beaches and sand dunes rolling down the Atlantic coast and pine and eucalyptus woods – a paradise for bird watchers, nature lovers, artists, photographers and soul-searchers. The lovely golden sandy beach is a short stroll from the house. The local beaches are great for swimming and water sports – Arrifana and Amado attract surfers from all over the world. The area has interesting market towns and unique fishing villages worth exploring, not least the picturesque Moorish town of Aljezur, only a ten minute drive away, as well as Monchique, noted for its mountains and famed health spa, and the ancient coastal town of Lagos. 

It is 50 miles from Faro International Airport, an easy hour`s drive north west coast on the A22 motorway. While a car is essential here, there is a reliable bus service Faro/Aljezur/Faro if you prefer to take the scenery en route.
