DAY 1
A full day of adventure awaits you! Book a guided 4x4 tour to the Natural Park of the Guadiana Valley and appreciate rare fauna and flora, picturesque little villages, wonderful local gastronomy and amazing scenery, including the dramatic Pulo do Lobo waterfall. Camp in the wild near the river (permission should be sought), lay back and genuinely enjoy the stunning outdoors and the sparkling stars!

DAY 2
Wake up at dawn at the beautiful sound of the water stream and take off on an exciting paragliding trip or go on a breathtaking guided walking trail through the enchanting Natural Park of the Guadiana Valley, carpeted with spectacular flowers, spotting rare bird species.
